Workshops and Events

C/C++ Workshop

This workshop suits best to all those beginners to programming and those coding enthusiast who want to learn how to make their programs solve some interesting and amazing programming problems.

Data Structures Workshop

Get acquainted with the concepts of Arrays, Pointers, Stacks & Queues, Linked list, Trees, graphs, Graph Traversal Algorithms and much more.

IoT Workshop

To spread awareness and cognizance about this aspect of internet which excels in both novelty and utility, IEEE BIT Mesra in association with i3indiya conducted a workshop.

Digital Electronics Workshop

This workshop is an effort to help the students learn the basics of digital electronics. Be it designing those cool chips, making your own embedded systems or creating your own robot, it all starts from here.

Da Vinci Code

A simple code deciphering game in which the participants had to solve puzzles, problems and clues and reach a final destination.

Up In The Air

A fun filled design based event where the participants explore the mysteries of flight and create a device from the raw materials provided that can remain airborne to cover the maximum distance.

A to Z Quiz

An open invitation to all the quizzers, doesn't matter the type, the quiz covers up all the topics of a normal human being.

Cash Flow

All those business interested geeks are invited here! Learn more about the buy-sell period of the stocks and take a dive into the world of Stock Trading!

IEEE Day - Online Debate

A day to celebrate innovations, technologies, engineering and some amazing ideas which have capabilities to change the world and we conduct an online Tech Debate.

Into The Future

'Into the Future', an online Ideation challenge which asks you to predict ideas of technologies of the future, how the'll surface and in what way would they dent the world.
