Workshops and Events

C/C++ Workshop

IEEE BIT Mesra conducts C/C++ workshop for all the beginners in programming. The workshop spans over three days and all the basic concepts of C/C++ programming are taught.

Data Structures Workshop

IEEE BIT Mesra conducts 3 day workshop on Data Structures with an aim to provide a good grip and in depth knowledge of the subject. The 2nd year and 3rd year members of the club teach the concepts of stacks, queues, linked lists, trees and graphs.

IoT Workshop

A workshoop on Internet of Things was presented by IEEE, BIT Mesra in collaboration with i3indya.

Cash Flow

Cash Flow is a virtual stock trading fun event. The event involves buying and selling of stocks based on some news and predictions.

Da Vinci Code

Da Vinci Code is a technical treasure hunt where participants need to solve various riddles in order to reach different destinations spread across the campus.

A 2 Z Quiz

The quiz that covers everything. From Sports to entertainment, from music to dance and from fashion to industries.
